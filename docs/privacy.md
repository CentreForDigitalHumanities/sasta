# SASTA Privacy Policy

SASTA is developed by the Centre for Digital Humanties at Utrecht University. This privacy policy explains how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

## What data do we collect?

On account creation, the following data is collected:

- Email address and username

On uploading transcripts, the following data is collected:

- The raw input file

## How will we use your data?
Account information:
- Account information is used for authenticating the user. Email address may be used in case communication that is important for the user. No information or marketing communication is sent.

Uploaded transcripts:

- Transcript is processed for analysis in a few steps, all of which are necessary for the analysis of the uploaded transcripts:
    - (optional) convert text documents (such as Word `.docx files) to flat text documents
    - (optional) convert flat text documents to CHAT documents
    - parse the documents using Alpino to generate treebank files
    - postprocess the treebank files to enable SASTA analysis
    - perform analysis on the processed treebank files
- Transcripts and the derived files are only used to perform analysis
- With specific permission, your transcript, derived files, and performed analyses may be shared with the researchers involved in the project, to test and enchance the application. See (LINK)

## How do we store your data?
Your data is stored securely on servers managed by the Centre for Digital Humanities, Utrecht University. The data is only accesible to developers and server technicians of the Centre for Digital Humanities.
Care is taken to keep the security of the servers up-to-date, to prevent any accidental leaks or malignant data theft.
The data is kept for an indefinite period, no data will be deleted on our initiative.


## What are your data protection rights?
We would like to make sure that you are fully aware of your data protection rights. Every user is entitles to the following:

- **The right to access** - You have the right to request a copy of your personal data.
- **The right to rectification** - You have the right to request us to correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request us to complete any information you believe is incomplete.
- **The right to erasure** - You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
- **The right to restrict processing** - You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
- **The right to object to processing** - You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
- **The right to data portability** - You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under cetrain conditions.


If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: cdh@uu.nl

## Cookies
Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology

For further information, visit [allaboutcookies.org](https://www.allaboutcookies.org).

### What cookies do we use?
We use use functional cookies to keep your login information between sessions. This ensures that you do not need to login every time you visit the website.
No advertisiing or data tracking cookies are used.

### How to manage cookies
You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, in a few cases, some of our website features may not function as a result.

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us.
